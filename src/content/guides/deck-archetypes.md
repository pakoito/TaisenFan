# Sangokushi Taisen Ten - Deck Archetypes

> **Terminology Note:** This guide uses official English translations. Key terms: POW (combat power), INT (intelligence), MP (morale points for Skills), HP (unit health), Skill (special abilities), Sage (non-combat advisor cards), Command (team buff Skills), Trait (passive abilities like Ambush, Valor, Charm).

## Overview

This guide covers the standard deck archetypes for general play, WiFi matches, and VS mode. These are the "meta" deck types that form the backbone of competitive play.

For DUEL mode-specific strategies against CPU opponents, see [Deck Strategies](/guides/deck-strategies). For campaign progression, see [Campaign Guide](/guides/campaign).

## Deck Building Fundamentals

### The 8-Cost Rule
Every deck must total exactly 8 cost. Common distributions:
- **5-card:** 2.5/2/1.5/1/1 or 2/2/1.5/1.5/1
- **4-card:** 3/2.5/1.5/1 or 2.5/2.5/2/1
- **6-card:** 2/2/1/1/1/1 or 2/1.5/1.5/1/1/1

### Faction Synergy
- **Single faction:** Maximum MP is 12
- **Two factions:** Maximum MP is 9
- **Three+ factions:** Maximum MP is 6

Matching Lord factions to your Sage also affects Ability gauge speed.

### Gameplay Considerations
- **Touch controls limit cavalry management.** Stick to 1-2 Horse units maximum.
- **HP recovery is slow.** Regen trait and recovery Skills are nearly mandatory.
- **Ram units revive at full HP**, making them exceptionally durable.
- **Thrust attacks are forgiving.** Easy to execute consistently.
- **CPU positions are fixed** in single-player modes, so you can memorize Ambush locations.

## Faction Reference

Each faction has distinct strengths in unit types, Skills, and playstyle. Understanding these helps when building decks.

### Wei (Red)

**Strength:** Horse units | **Damage Type:** Water

Wei is the kingdom founded by Cao Cao, the cunning warlord who dominated northern China. Wei fields the most Horse units in the game, and most of them are excellent.

**Unit Composition:**
- **Horse:** Abundant and powerful. Many decks run Horse units exclusively.
- **Bow:** Fewer than Wu, but individually just as strong.
- **Spear:** Very limited selection with modest stats.
- **Sword/Ram:** None. Wei has no Sword or Ram units.

**Tactical Style:**
- Mobility-focused. Use Horse speed to strike where enemies are weak.
- Strong debuff Skills like Dispel and various weakening effects.
- Water damage Skills: Water Bane and Great Flood.
- Command Skills like Wei War Order boost entire armies.

**Affinity Types:** Wei Lords and Sages only have Heaven and Earth affinities. No Man affinity exists in Wei.

**Special Note:** Wei is the only faction with Legend (LE) rarity cards. These feature Souten Kouro crossover artwork and voice lines.

### Shu (Green)

**Strength:** Spear units | **Damage Type:** Lightning

Shu is the kingdom founded by Liu Bei, the benevolent ruler who sought to restore the Han dynasty. Shu excels at Spear warfare, and its Spear Lords have unusually high stats for their Cost.

**Unit Composition:**
- **Spear:** Overwhelmingly numerous with excellent Cost efficiency.
- **Horse:** Good selection, rivaling Wei in quality if not quantity.
- **Bow:** Very limited with modest stats.

**Tactical Style:**
- Defensive with strong Spear Counter potential against Horse units.
- Powerful Command Skills: Liu Bei Virtue, Loyal Order.
- Lightning damage Skills: Lightning, Judgment Bolt, Guard Thunder.
- Debuff options include Provoke and Chain Ploy.

**Affinity Types:** Shu Lords and Sages only have Heaven and Man affinities. No Earth affinity exists in Shu.

### Wu (Blue)

**Strength:** Bow units | **Damage Type:** Fire

Wu is the kingdom ruled by the Sun family across three generations. Wu has the most Bow units and many Lords with the Fence Trait, making them defensive specialists.

**Unit Composition:**
- **Bow:** Most numerous faction for this unit type.
- **Spear:** Second most common, quite capable.
- **Horse:** Very few, but each one is viable.
- **Ram:** None. Wu has no Ram units.

**Tactical Style:**
- Defensive. Fences slow enemy advances while Bow units deal damage from range.
- Fire damage Skills: Fire Ploy, Wu Flames, Chibi Inferno, Yiling Flames.
- Strong Command Skills but notably lacks debuff-focused Lords.
- Often runs mixed Horse/Spear/Bow compositions.

**Affinity Types:** Wu Lords and Sages only have Man and Earth affinities. No Heaven affinity exists in Wu.

### Rogue (Gray)

**Strength:** Versatile | **Damage Type:** All (fire, water, lightning)

Rogue represents the independent warlords, bandits, and heroes who didn't align with the three kingdoms. This faction has the most variety but the least internal synergy.

**Unit Composition:**
- **Horse:** Slightly more common than other types.
- **Bow:** Second most common.
- **Spear:** Limited but each Lord is excellent.
- **Ram:** Four Ram Lords, more than all other factions combined.

**Tactical Style:**
- Swarm tactics. Many 1-Cost Lords with the Undying Trait enable large armies.
- Unorthodox Command Skills that damage your own castle for power boosts.
- Access to all three damage types: fire, water, and lightning.
- Debuffs include Death Ploy and Block Retreat.
- Unique Skills like Assassin Toxin blend damage and debuff.

**Affinity Types:** Rogue has all three affinities (Heaven, Earth, Man) because its Sages don't share a unified attribute pattern.

**Build Warning:** Rogue has only one 2-Cost Lord, and he's not particularly strong. Decks built purely from Rogue tend to have awkward Cost distributions.

## Command Decks

Team-wide buff strategies built around powerful Command Skills.

---

### Lord's Appeal

**Faction:** Wei

SR Cao Cao's Command Skill grants POW+5 to all allies in range for 9.5 counts. The longest-lasting Command in the game, though with a narrow range.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Cao Cao | 2.5 | Horse | Heaven | Ambush/Charm | 8/10 | Lord's Appeal |

**Core Strategy:**
Wei excels at Horse units and low-MP utility Skills. Build around Horse-heavy compositions and use Cao Cao's high INT to survive Ambushes. The long Skill duration lets you maintain pressure while teammates use support Skills like Deny, Dispel, or Flash buffs.

**Deck Patterns:**
- **Pure Command:** Maximize POW with Horse units, crush opponents with raw stats
- **Combo Type:** Pair with Deny/Dispel for control, or Separation for disruption
- **Dual Threat:** Include damage Skills like Water Bane as alternate win conditions

**Sample Deck:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Cao Cao | 2.5 | Horse | Heaven | Ambush/Charm | 8/10 | Lord's Appeal |
| R Pang De | 2 | Horse | Earth | - | 9/1 | Kamikaze |
| UC Cao Ren | 1.5 | Horse | Heaven | - | 6/5 | Speed Tactics |
| UC Yue Jin | 1 | Horse | Heaven | - | 4/3 | Power Tactics |
| R Jia Xu | 1 | Horse | Heaven | Ambush | 1/9 | Divide Ploy |

**Total:** POW 28 / INT 28

**Strengths:**
- Long Skill duration for sustained pressure
- High INT composition resists damage Skills
- Flexible between pure aggression and control

**Weaknesses:**
- Narrow Skill range makes positioning critical
- Struggles against speed-boosting Commands
- Horse-heavy builds lack siege power

**Sage Recommendations:** Sima Yi (Primal One for burst), Guo Jia (Charge Formation), Xun Yu (Elite Formation)

### Great Virtue

**Faction:** Shu

SR Liu Bei's Command grants POW+5 with an exceptionally wide range, perfect for Spear-heavy compositions. Liu Bei himself has Undying, Charm, and Regen, making him surprisingly durable.

**Core Strategy:**
The wide range lets you buff spread-out Spear formations. Liu Bei's low POW means he needs protection, but his Undying trait means he recovers quickly if killed. Pair with Spears for defense and 1-2 Horses for mobility.

**Sample Deck:**

### Young King

**Faction:** Wu

R Sun Quan's Command grants POW+4 to Wu allies. Lower buff than other Commands, but Sun Quan brings Fence and Charm while being a Bow unit for backline support.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| R Sun Quan | 1.5 | Bow | Earth | Fence/Charm | 4/7 | Young King Art |

**Core Strategy:**
Wu excels at Fence placement and Bow support. Use Fences to control enemy movement while Bows whittle down approaching units. Sun Quan's low cost leaves room for high-POW attackers.

**Strengths:**
- Fence control limits enemy options
- Bow support chips away at enemies safely
- Lower cost key card allows flexible builds

**Weaknesses:**
- Lower POW buff than other Commands
- Fence-dependent strategies are predictable
- Struggles if Fences are destroyed early

**Sage Recommendations:** Zhou Yu (HP Recovery/Inferno), Lu Xun (Revive/Chain), Lu Meng (Fence Rebuild/Far Bow)

### Divine Speed

**Faction:** Wei

SR Zhang Liao's Command grants POW+3 and speed boost to Horse units, enabling devastating Chain Charge attacks. Requires excellent Horse control.

**Key Card:**

|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Zhang Liao | 2.5 | Horse | Heaven | Valor | 9/7 | Speed Order |

**Core Strategy:**
Build an all-Horse deck. Activate Speed Order and use Chain Charges to rapidly eliminate enemies one by one. The speed boost lets you disengage before Spears can Counter. Requires high skill to avoid Counters during speed-boosted movement.

**Sample Deck:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Zhang Liao | 2.5 | Horse | Heaven | Valor | 9/7 | Speed Order |

| R Jia Xu | 1 | Horse | Heaven | Ambush | 1/9 | Divide Ploy |

**Total:** POW 29 / INT 25

**Strengths:**
- Overwhelming mobility
- Chain Charges devastate isolated units
- Zhang Liao's Valor helps in duels

**Weaknesses:**
- Spear-heavy decks are a nightmare
- Requires excellent Horse control on touchscreen
- All-Horse means low siege power

**Note:** Managing multiple speed-boosted Horses is extremely difficult. Consider limiting to 4 cards maximum.

**Sage Recommendations:** Guo Jia (Charge Formation), Xun You (Chain debuff), Sima Yi (Primal One)

### Loyal Order

**Faction:** Shu

SR Guan Yu's Command grants POW+3 plus HP recovery that exceeds maximum. The recovery persists after the Skill ends, making it excellent for sustained pushes.

**Core Strategy:**
Guan Yu's massive stats let him dominate without Skills. Use Loyal Order to push into enemy territory with overheal, then maintain pressure with Regen while waiting for the next Skill. The HP recovery lets you either lead aggressively or recover after taking damage.

**Sample Deck:**

## Dance Decks

Persistent buff effects that last as long as the dancer survives. High risk, high reward.

---

### Healing Dance

**Faction:** Shu or mixed

UC Lady Gan's Dance continuously heals all allies. Powerful when you can protect the dancer, but she's fragile at POW 1.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| UC Lady Gan | 1 | Sword | Heaven | Charm | 1/4 | Healing Dance |

**Core Strategy:**
Build with high-POW units and at least one Fence. Position the dancer safely behind Fences and frontline units. The continuous healing lets your army stay in the field indefinitely. MP cost is 7, so timing the dance is critical.

**Sample Deck:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| R Ma Chao | 2 | Horse | Man | Charm/Valor | 8/3 | 1000-Man Slayer |
| R Pang Tong | 1 | Spear | Man | Ambush | 1/9 | Chain Ploy+ |
| UC Lady Gan | 1 | Sword | Heaven | Charm | 1/4 | Healing Dance |
| SR Zuo Ci | 1.5 | Sword | Heaven | Ambush/Undying | 4/9 | Transform Art |
| C Li Guo | 1.5 | Bow | Man | Fence | 6/3 | Pure Evil |
| C Zhang Liang | 1 | Sword | Man | - | 5/1 | YT Swarm |

**Total:** POW 25 / INT 29

**Strengths:**
- Infinite sustain while dancer lives
- Counters attrition strategies
- Strong against decks lacking burst damage

**Weaknesses:**
- Dancer is extremely fragile
- Far Bow strategies hard-counter this
- MP 7 cost means vulnerable period after dancing

**Counter Strategy:** Target the dancer immediately. Damage Skills, long-range Bows, and speed-boosted charges can reach her behind defenses.

**Sage Recommendations:** Jia Xu (Revive/Elite Line), Pang Tong (Chain), Mi Zhu (Valor Formation)

### Flying Heaven Dance

**Faction:** Wei or mixed

UC Cai Wenji's Dance boosts all ally movement speed. Devastating with Horse units but makes everyone vulnerable to Counter attacks.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| UC Cai Wenji | 1 | Horse | Earth | Charm | 1/7 | Heaven Dance |

**Core Strategy:**
Build a Horse-heavy deck. Activate the dance and use superior mobility to run circles around enemies. However, the speed boost means even non-Horse units take Double Counter damage from Spears. Avoid dancing against Spear-heavy opponents.

**Strengths:**
- Overwhelming mobility
- Enables hit-and-run tactics
- Horse units become nearly untouchable

**Weaknesses:**
- Spears become deadly to everyone
- Provoke Skills are devastating
- Dancer must survive or strategy collapses

**Note:** Managing multiple high-speed units is very difficult. This deck is expert-level.

**Sage Recommendations:** Xun You (Chain), Guo Jia (Charge Formation)

## Superweapon Decks

One powerful unit carries the team while cheap supports enable them.

---

### Lu Bu Swarm

**Faction:** Rogue or mixed

SR Lu Bu's POW 10+ and Unrivaled Skill make him a one-man army. Surround him with cheap units for support.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Lu Bu | 3 | Horse | Heaven | Valor | 10+/1 | Unrivaled |

**Core Strategy:**
Lu Bu alone can defeat most units. Use cheap supports to clear Ambushes, provide walls, and siege while Lu Bu dominates combat. Unrivaled costs MP 6 and grants massive POW, speed, and HP recovery for a short duration.

**Deck Patterns:**
- **Swarm (3/1/1/1/1/1):** Maximum support units, Lu Bu does all fighting
- **Balance (3/1.5/1.5/1/1):** Some combat capability beyond Lu Bu
- **Five Pecks (with C Zhang Lu):** Revive Lu Bu with Five Pecks Way if he falls

**Sample Deck (Swarm):**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Lu Bu | 3 | Horse | Heaven | Valor | 10+/1 | Unrivaled |
| C Zhang Liang | 1 | Sword | Man | - | 5/1 | YT Swarm |
| C Cheng Yuanzhi | 1 | Spear | Heaven | Undying | 3/1 | Power Tactics |
| C Pei Yuanshao | 1 | Horse | Earth | Undying | 3/1 | Power Tactics |
| C Zhang Lu | 1 | Bow | Man | - | 2/6 | Five Pecks Way |
| UC Yu Ji | 1 | Sword | Heaven | Ambush | 1/7 | Water Bane |

**Total:** POW 24+ / INT 17

**Strengths:**
- Lu Bu dominates 1v1 fights
- Cheap units provide utility and siege
- Unrivaled can turn any fight

**Weaknesses:**
- Lu Bu's INT 1 makes him vulnerable to debuffs
- If Lu Bu dies without Five Pecks, you're in trouble
- Struggles against coordinated focus fire

**Sage Recommendations:** Chen Gong (Revive/INT Formation for longer Unrivaled), Jia Xu (Elite Line)

## Disruption Decks

Control and debuff focused strategies.

---

### Death Ploy

**Faction:** Rogue or mixed

R Jia Xu's Death Ploy prevents enemy revival, making kills permanent during its effect.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| R Jia Xu | 1.5 | Horse | Heaven | Ambush | 4/9 | Death Ploy |

**Core Strategy:**
Use Death Ploy on key enemy units, then eliminate them. They cannot revive for an extended period, giving you numerical advantage. Often paired with Lu Bu or other high-damage units for guaranteed kills.

**Sage Recommendations:** Chen Gong (INT Formation), Mi Heng (Horse Fool)

---

### 8 Trigrams

**Faction:** Shu

SR Zhuge Liang's 8 Trigrams has different effects based on how many allies are in range. Extremely versatile but complex.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Zhuge Liang | 1.5 | Spear | Man | Ambush/Charm/Regen | 3/10 | 8 Trigrams |

**Skill Effects by Target Count:**
- **1 unit:** POW+1, HP recovery to 200%
- **2 units:** POW+5, speed x1.8
- **3 units:** POW+4, immune to enemy Skills
- **4+ units:** POW+3, gradual HP recovery

**Core Strategy:**
Adapt to the situation. Use 1-unit for clutch defense, 2-unit for Horse burst, 3-unit for Skill immunity pushes. Can double-cast for powerful combos like 2+2 (POW+10, 3x speed) or 3+3 (POW+8, Skill immunity).

**Sample Deck:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Zhuge Liang | 1.5 | Spear | Man | Ambush/Charm/Regen | 3/10 | 8 Trigrams |
| UC Zhang Fei | 2 | Spear | Heaven | Valor | 9/1 | Power Tactics |
| R Ma Chao | 2 | Horse | Man | Charm/Valor | 8/3 | 1000-Man Slayer |
| R Xu Shu | 1.5 | Horse | Heaven | Ambush | 4/9 | Lightning |
| C Zhao Lei | 1 | Spear | Heaven | Fence | 2/7 | Wheel Tutor |

**Total:** POW 26 / INT 30

**Strengths:**
- Extreme flexibility
- Double-cast combos are devastating
- Skill immunity counters many strategies

**Weaknesses:**
- Complex to master
- Lower POW buffs than pure Commands
- Requires MP management skill

**Sage Recommendations:** Mi Zhu (cannot use Sage Zhuge Liang with card Zhuge Liang), Pang Tong, Fa Zheng

## Opening Rush Decks

End the game in the opening phase with overwhelming force.

---

### 36 Formula

**Faction:** Any (typically mixed)

The highest total POW deck possible: 36. Rush the enemy before they can respond.

**Sample Deck:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| R Pang De | 2 | Horse | Earth | - | 9/1 | Kamikaze |
| UC Gan Ning | 2 | Bow | Man | - | 9/1 | Wu Valor |
| UC Zhang Fei | 2 | Spear | Heaven | Valor | 9/1 | Power Tactics |
| UC Zhou Cang | 1 | Spear | Man | Valor | 4/2 | Power Tactics |
| C Zhang Liang | 1 | Sword | Man | - | 5/1 | YT Swarm |

**Total:** POW 36 / INT 6

**Core Strategy:**
Rush immediately. Your raw POW advantage crushes early game. Use Zhou Yu Sage with Inferno to reveal Ambushes if needed. Must win before enemy MP accumulates.

**Strengths:**
- Overwhelming early game power
- Simple execution
- Catches unprepared opponents

**Weaknesses:**
- No INT means Ambushes are lethal
- No answer to mid-game Skills
- If rush fails, you lose

**Sage Recommendations:** Zhou Yu (Inferno reveals Ambushes), Zhuge Liang (Elite Formation)

---

### Opening Elite

**Faction:** Any (Man affinity focused)

Use Elite Formation from turn 1 for 15+ counts of POW+2 on all units.

**Core Strategy:**
Stack Man affinity units, activate Elite Formation immediately, and rush with sustained POW boost. Unlike 36 Formula, this has staying power into mid-game.

**Sage Recommendations:** Zhuge Liang (must be Man affinity focused)

| R Xiao Qiao | 1.5 | Sword | Man | Fence/Charm | 2/5 | Meteor Ritual |
| R Da Qiao | 1 | Sword | Man | Charm | 1/5 | Grand Meteor |

**Core Strategy:**
Build maximum Fences, stall the entire game, then drop Meteor for castle damage. Extremely defensive but predictable. Grand Meteor deals ~99% castle damage if it connects.

**Strengths:**
- Win without ever attacking
- Fence-heavy defense is strong
- Forces opponents to rush

**Weaknesses:**
- Very predictable strategy
- Bow-heavy decks can snipe the caster
- Siege Camp (R Gao Shun) destroys Fences instantly

**Sage Recommendations:** Zhou Yu (Inferno for defense), Lu Xun (Chain), Lu Meng (Fence Rebuild)

### Thunder Chain / Provocation Chain

**Faction:** Shu

R Pang Tong's Chain Ploy+ immobilizes enemies, then R Xu Shu's Lightning or R Jiang Wei's Provoke finishes them.

**Key Cards:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |

| R Jiang Wei | 2 | Spear | Man | Regen | 7/7 | Provoke |

**Core Strategy:**
Chain Ploy+ slows enemies to a crawl. Follow up with Lightning to eliminate grouped enemies, or Provoke to drag enemies into Counter range. Pang Tong Sage with Iron Chain Formation stacks for near-total immobilization.

### Yellow Turban

**Faction:** Rogue (Yellow Turban subfaction)

SR Zhang Jiao's YT Rebellion revives all dead allies and buffs them, but he dies when using it.

**Key Card:**
| Card | Cost | Type | Affinity | Traits | POW/INT | Skill |
|------|------|------|----------|--------|---------|-------|
| SR Zhang Jiao | 2.5 | Horse | Heaven | Fence/Regen | 5/9 | YT Rebellion |

**Core Strategy:**
Build around cheap Yellow Turban units with Undying trait (C Zhang Liang, C Cheng Yuanzhi, C Pei Yuanshao). Let them die, then use YT Rebellion to revive everyone with POW+3 buff. **Sage Recommendations:** Zhang Jiao (Sage)
