# Campaign Merchant Reference

Each chapter has merchants selling Lords at three price tiers. This is a complete listing of available Lords by chapter and stage.

> **Terminology Note:** Card rarities: C (Common), UC (Uncommon), R (Rare), SR (Super Rare), EX (Extra). Factions: Wei (魏), Wu (呉), Shu (蜀), Rogue (群). See [Beginner's Guide](/guides/beginners) for full terminology.

> **Tip:** If you select starting Lords that would normally appear in a merchant's inventory, they count as "already owned" and won't appear for purchase. Use this to guarantee specific cards from the merchant pool.
