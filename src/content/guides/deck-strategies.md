# Sangokushi Taisen Ten - Deck Strategies

This guide covers proven deck archetypes for achieving S-rank in DUEL mode, particularly on Extreme difficulty. These strategies were documented by the Japanese wiki community and exploit AI behavior patterns rather than trying to match the enemy's overwhelming stats.

For stage listings and general DUEL information, see the [DUEL Guide](/guides/duel).

> **Terminology Note:** This guide uses descriptive English terms for readability. In the game's UI: cavalry = Horse, archers = Bow, spearmen = Spear, siege units = Ram. Stats are POW (attack), INT (intelligence), HP (health), MP (morale/skill meter), SPD (speed). Character Skills are called "Skills" and Sage special abilities are called "Abilities."

---

## CPU Behavior Patterns

Understanding how the AI works is essential for exploiting it:

- **CPU waits for 100% HP after dying** before attacking again
- **CPU prioritizes dancers above all else** - they'll charge through spears and poison to kill a dancing unit
- **CPU favors single-target Buffs** over Command skills
- **CPU siege units just march forward** - easy to predict
- **CPU positions are fixed** - learn where Ambushes hide
- **CPU triggers Rally** when down to 1 unit and you have 2+ in their territory

---

## Deck Building Principles

1. **One deck cannot clear all Extreme stages** - accept this and build specialized decks
2. **Match affinities** for fast Ability gauge fill
3. **Bring counters** to specific enemy threats (Deny for Inferno users, speed skills for paralysis)
4. **Consider siege power** - you need to actually destroy the castle, not just kill units

## Strategy 1: Grand Meteor

**Concept:** Protect R Da Qiao while she casts Grand Meteor. Minimize unit deaths while dealing massive castle damage.

**Works best against:** Grand Meteor Maidens (Extreme Stage 19)

**Core Cards:**
- R Da Qiao (dancer with Grand Meteor ritual)
- SR Lu Meng or R Xiahou Yuan (Wild Shots for defense)
- Paralyze Arrow users for crowd control
- High-POW Horse for intercepting threats
- R Xun Yu if facing R Zhou Yu (counter his Inferno with Deny)

**Sage:** SR Zhou Yu with Inferno (positioned at top-right)

**Strategy:**
1. Position your units at bottom-left, fences protecting Da Qiao
2. Wait for MP 7, then start Grand Meteor
3. Enemy rushes Da Qiao. Use Horse to intercept and melee their archers
4. Watch for enemy R Zhou Yu's Inferno. Position R Xun Yu to Deny it
5. Burn enemy Da Qiao with Inferno
6. Wild Shots + Horse charges to clean up

**Adaptations:**
- Against many bows: Add 2-cost high-POW Horse to charge and melee them
- Against many cavalry: Use Long Spear tactics to protect Da Qiao
- The AI always prioritizes killing dancers, so position defenses accordingly

## Strategy 2: Ambush Lance

**Concept:** Line up high-INT Ambush units to instantly kill low-INT enemies, then siege rush with C Chen Lan.

**Works best against:** Tiger Fool Evil (Extreme Stage 18) - all low-INT brutes

**Core Cards:**
- 6× High-INT Ambush units (line up on one side)
- C Chen Lan (Ram with 3× speed skill)
- 1× Horse for cleanup

**Strategy:**
1. Line up Ambush units on the right side
2. Send C Chen Lan behind them toward the enemy castle
3. Enemy charges through your Ambush line, taking massive INT-based damage
4. Most enemies die instantly to Ambush reveals
5. Use Horse to mop up any surviving archers
6. Chen Lan's siege + your revealed Ambush units all siege together
7. Often achieves castle fall even if Rally triggers

**Why it works:** Enemy army is 16-cost but all low-INT. Ambush damage scales with your INT, so one reveal can one-shot multiple enemies.

## Strategy 3: Field Dance Long Spear

**Concept:** The AI prioritizes killing dancers above all else. Use Field Dance as bait while Spear Flash shreds anyone who approaches.

**Works best against:** Grand General (Extreme Stage 8), Extreme Healing Maidens (Extreme Stage 13)

**Core Cards:**
- C Lady Mi (Field Dance)
- R Jiang Wei, UC Ling Tong, UC Guan Ping, C Dong Xi (all Man affinity for fast Formation charge)
- Fences to protect early game

**Sage:** Fa Zheng (Spear Flash)

**Sample Deck:** R Jiang Wei, UC Ling Tong, UC Guan Ping, C Dong Xi, C Lady Mi

**Strategy:**
1. Early game: Hold position behind fences, use Thrust to poke or Long Spear skill at MP 3
2. When Ability gauge fills, activate Spear Flash
3. Immediately start Field Dance with Lady Mi
4. Enemy abandons all tactics and charges straight at Lady Mi
5. Four spear units with extended Thrust range massacre anyone approaching
6. Use Long Spear skill (MP 3) for ridiculous range extensions
7. Jiang Wei's Provoke pulls archers into Thrust range

**Weakness:** Struggles against high-bow compositions. Use a different strategy.

## Strategy 4: Opening Long Spear

**Concept:** Activate Spear Flash at battle start and steamroll with high-POW spears.

**Works best against:** Wei Emperor (Extreme Stage 16), Shu Emperor (Extreme Stage 20)

**Core Cards:**
- Multiple high-POW Spear units (all same affinity)
- Formation fills fast with matching affinities

**Sage:** Fa Zheng or Lu Meng (Spear Flash)

**Strategy:**
1. Position spears to avoid enemy Ambush locations (CPU positions are fixed)
2. Activate Spear Flash immediately when gauge fills
3. March forward. Extended Thrust range + high POW = instant kills
4. Even the 18-cost Shu Emperor army melts to this
5. Often achieves 40,000+ points due to speed and no losses

**Note:** Struggles against "Famous Wu Generals" (Extreme Stage 15) due to multiple high-POW bows.

## Strategy 5: Yellow Chain

**Concept:** Skip dangerous Ambush-heavy stages by using UC Huang Yueying's siege rush with Chain Device to ignore hidden enemies.

**Works best against:** Sleeping Dragon and Phoenix (Extreme Stage 3), Sima Yi Couple (Extreme Stage 2)

**Problem:** Stages like "Sleeping Dragon and Phoenix" have SR Zhuge Liang, R Pang Tong, R Xu Shu - three deadly Ambush units. Triggering them means death.

**Solution:** Don't fight hidden enemies. Use Chain to freeze visible enemies and rush the castle with Huang Yueying.

**Core Cards:**
- UC Huang Yueying (Charm, 1-cost Yellow Accel skill)
- 2× 1.5-cost Charm units (same affinity)
- 2× 2-cost Recruit units (Spear or Horse, same affinity)

**Sage:** Li Ru (Link Force - wider range than Iron Chain)

**Strategy:**
1. Never enter enemy territory with anyone except Huang Yueying
2. Fight only visible enemies near your territory (kill or chase off)
3. At MP ~3.5, deploy Huang Yueying from castle toward far side
4. At MP 4, use Yellow Accel (3× speed)
5. As Huang Yueying passes center, activate Link Force on visible enemies
6. Huang Yueying reaches castle and sieges
7. If all visible enemies are dead, skip the Chain entirely

**Sample Deck:** UC Huang Yueying, SR Sun Ren, DS Diao Chan, UC Huang Zhong, R Wei Yan, Sage: Li Ru

## Strategy 6: Horse Fool Ambush

**Concept:** Mi Heng's Deception reduces all enemies' INT by 7. Combined with your Ambush units, enemies die instantly when they step on you.

**Works best against:** Western Heroes (Extreme Stage 1), Jing Province Assault (Extreme Stage 4), Wu Emperor (Extreme Stage 6), Unrivaled (Extreme Stage 9), Four Fierce (Extreme Stage 12), Five Tiger Generals (Extreme Stage 14)

**Core Cards:**
- Multiple Earth affinity Ambush units
- C Chen Lan or other Ram for siege
- 1-2× Horse for cleanup after Ambush reveals
- SR Deng Ai (high-POW Ambush, doubles as wall)

**Sage:** Mi Heng (Deception) - mandatory for this strategy

**Sample Deck:** C Lei Bo, C Chen Lan, SR Zhang Chunhua, SR Deng Ai, R Jia Xu (Wei), UC Kuai Yue, C Lu Fan

**Strategy:**
1. Position Deception in enemy's path (left side if they have fences on right)
2. Line up Ambush units near city gate
3. Send Ram toward enemy castle
4. Enemy charges through Deception, loses INT
5. Enemy steps on Ambush, dies instantly
6. Use Horse to chase off survivors before they regroup
7. Ram + revealed Ambush units siege for fast castle fall

**Example: Jing Province Assault**
1. Attack from left side (avoid fences)
2. Position Deception left of center, in front of fences
3. Ambush units in order from left: Lei Bo, Zhang Chunhua, Deng Ai, Jia Xu
4. Ding Feng survives (higher INT). Use Horse charges to kill him
5. Even with Rally, Chen Lan's castle gate siege + other unit sieges finish it

## Strategy 7: Elite Opening

**Concept:** When Opening Rush lacks firepower, add Rally Elite to push total team POW to 43.

**Works best against:** Siege Camp (Extreme Stage 5), Nation-Toppling Maidens (Extreme Stage 10), Three Generations (Extreme Stage 11), Famous Wu Generals (Extreme Stage 15)

**Core Cards:**
- UC Yan Yan or UC Guan Ping
- R Zhang Fei
- C Zhang Liang
- C Chen Lan
- UC Gan Ning
- All Man affinity for fast Formation

**Sage:** SR Zhuge Liang (Rally Elite)

**Strategy:**
1. Learn fixed Ambush positions for the stage
2. Activate Rally Elite at battle start
3. With 43 total POW, just march forward
4. Kill everything, siege castle
5. Less complex than Long Spear but handles archers better

**Use this when:** Opening Long Spear fails because Rally triggers before you can finish sieging, or when facing bow-heavy compositions.

## Strategy 8: Opening Rush

**Concept:** A simpler version of Elite Opening using the "36 Formula" - rush the enemy at battle start before they can react.

**Works best against:** Most Normal and Hard stages, some Extreme stages with weaker compositions

**Core Cards:**
- High-POW units across all types
- Fast Ability gauge fill through matching affinities
- C Chen Lan for guaranteed siege damage

**Strategy:**
1. Deploy aggressively from the start
2. Use early Skills to buff your units
3. Overwhelm the enemy before they can use their Skills
4. Siege immediately after clearing resistance

**Note:** Less reliable on Extreme than specialized strategies, but good for stages without deadly Ambush units or overwhelming cost advantage.

## Scoring Optimization

When aiming for S-rank (30,000+ points):

- **Minimize deaths** - losing units hurts your score badly
- **Maximize enemy deaths** - but don't lose your own units doing it
- **Speed matters for castle fall bonus** - but only if you're fast
- **Manage Rally timing** - don't trigger it accidentally by having 2+ units in enemy territory when they're down to 1 unit
